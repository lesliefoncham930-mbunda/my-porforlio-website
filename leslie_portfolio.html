Breast Cancer Classification Using Decision Trees

A full machine learning pipeline on the Breast Cancer Wisconsin (Diagnostic) Dataset. Implements Decision Tree classification with GridSearchCV hyperparameter tuning, stratified cross-validation, feature importance analysis, and ROC-AUC evaluation. Achieved 94.19% test accuracy with a ROC-AUC of 0.9635 — demonstrating that interpretable ML can support medical diagnosis at a high level of confidence.
